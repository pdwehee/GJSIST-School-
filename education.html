About the College of Education

The Isaac Mulbah Lincoln College of Education at GJSIST is dedicated to training passionate, skilled, and innovative educators. Our programs focus on practical teaching, leadership, and community engagement to shape the next generation of teachers and leaders.

Programs Offered

Program Summary:
  • Foundations of Early Childhood Development
  • Child Psychology and Learning
  • Curriculum Design for Early Learners
  • Classroom Management & Assessment
  • Practicum in Early Childhood Settings

This program prepares graduates to teach and lead in early childhood education settings, focusing on holistic child development and innovative teaching strategies.

Program Summary:
  • Introduction to Education & Pedagogy
  • General Science & Mathematics for Teachers
  • Educational Technology
  • Classroom Communication Skills
  • Teaching Practicum

This diploma equips future educators with broad teaching skills for primary and junior secondary levels, emphasizing practical classroom experience and foundational knowledge.
