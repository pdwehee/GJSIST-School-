About the College

The College of Medical Sciences at GJSIST is dedicated to training skilled, compassionate, and innovative healthcare professionals. Our programs combine rigorous academics with hands-on clinical experience, preparing graduates to meet the health needs of Liberia and beyond.

Programs Offered

Medical Laboratory Technology

Gain expertise in laboratory diagnostics, clinical testing, and biomedical analysis. Graduates are prepared for careers in hospitals, clinics, and research labs.

Physician Assistantship

Train to become a vital member of the healthcare team, providing patient care, diagnosis, and treatment under physician supervision.

Midwifery (pending accreditation)

Prepare for a rewarding career supporting maternal and child health. Our midwifery program is designed to meet national and international standards.
