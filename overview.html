Overview of GJSIST

Empowering Liberia’s Next Generation of Leaders and Innovators

Our Mission & Vision

Glory Joyce Stanley Institute of Science and Technology (GJSIST) is dedicated to providing affordable, high-quality education that prepares students to excel in science, technology, and business fields. Founded on principles of academic excellence and innovation, we equip learners with the skills, knowledge, and ethical foundation required for leadership roles locally and globally.

Academic Excellence

Our programs are developed in collaboration with industry professionals and education experts to ensure relevance, rigor, and alignment with international standards. We foster a hands-on learning environment through practical labs, internships, and community engagement.

Community & Partnership

GJSIST is deeply committed to community development and partnerships that create opportunities for students and contribute to Liberia’s socio-economic growth. We maintain strong links with businesses, government, and global institutions to enhance learning and career prospects.

Student Success

Our student-centered approach emphasizes mentorship, career guidance, and leadership training, empowering our graduates to become changemakers in their communities and professions.

About GJSIST

GJSIST is committed to delivering quality and affordable education to empower the next generation of leaders and innovators in Liberia and beyond.
