International Students

GJSIST welcomes students from around the world! We are committed to supporting your academic journey in Liberia.

  • Certified copies of academic transcripts and certificates (with English translation if needed).
  • Proof of English proficiency (TOEFL, IELTS, or equivalent) if your previous education was not in English.
  • Copy of valid passport and recent passport-sized photographs.
  • Completed GJSIST international application form.
  • Proof of financial support or scholarship (if applicable).
  • Visa and immigration requirements as per Liberian law.

Our International Student Office will guide you through the application, arrival, and orientation process. For questions, email international@gjsist.edu.lr.

Admission Requirements

To join GJSIST, applicants must meet the following criteria:

  • Completed Senior High School or equivalent with passing grades in core subjects.
  • Official transcripts and certificates.
  • Two letters of recommendation from teachers or professionals.
  • Completed application form and payment of application fee.
  • Meeting specific program prerequisites if applicable.

Application Process

  1. Fill out the online application form available on the Student Portal.
  2. Submit all required documents including transcripts, certificates, and letters of recommendation.
  3. Pay the non-refundable application fee at the finance office or online.
  4. Attend an interview if shortlisted.
  5. Receive admission decision via email or portal notification.
