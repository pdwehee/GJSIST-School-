About GJSIST

Committed to Excellence in Education and Innovation

Overview

Glory Joyce Stanley Institute of Science and Technology (GJSIST) is a leading institution in Liberia dedicated to providing quality, affordable, and relevant education in science, technology, and business disciplines. Established with a vision to empower the next generation of leaders and innovators, GJSIST integrates academic excellence with practical experience, fostering a learning environment that prepares students for the competitive global landscape.

Our curriculum is designed in partnership with industry experts and educational authorities to ensure relevance and alignment with global standards. We prioritize student success through hands-on learning, internships, and mentorship programs.

Founder's Message

Glory Joyce Stanley

Glory Joyce Stanley, the visionary founder of GJSIST, is a respected educator and leader dedicated to transforming education in Liberia. With decades of experience in academic leadership and community development, she has been instrumental in establishing the institute as a beacon of quality education in the region.

Her commitment to empowering youth and fostering innovation has been the cornerstone of GJSIST's mission. Under her guidance, the institute continues to expand its programs and partnerships, providing students with opportunities to excel both locally and internationally.
