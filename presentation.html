GJSIST School Website

Presentation & Usage Summary

Glory Joyce Stanley Institute of Science and Technology

Purpose of the Website

  • Provide information about GJSIST, its mission, and academic offerings
  • Showcase programs, admissions, news, and campus life
  • Enable prospective students to learn about and apply to the institute
  • Serve as a resource hub for students, parents, and staff

Key Features

  • Modern, responsive design using Bootstrap 5
  • Professional navigation bar with dropdown menus for About, Programs, Admissions
  • Hero section with impactful welcome message and call-to-action buttons
  • News & Events, Testimonials, and Discover sections for engagement
  • Admissions and contact information easily accessible
  • Quick links and social media integration in the footer

User Experience

  • Easy navigation with clear menus and dropdowns
  • Mobile-friendly and accessible on all devices
  • Visually appealing sections with images, cards, and calls to action
  • Consistent color scheme and branding throughout

Content Overview

  • Home: Welcome, About, Opening Soon, News, Programs, Admissions, Testimonials, Discover, Contact Preview
  • About: Institute overview, mission, founder messages, finance, registrar
  • Programs: Bachelor, Associate, Business, Medical, Education, Library, Academic Calendar
  • Admissions: How to apply, requirements, scholarships, international applicants
  • Portal, Contact, Gallery: Student portal, contact form, campus gallery

Technologies Used

  • HTML5, CSS3, Bootstrap 5.3.3
  • Bootstrap Icons, Font Awesome, Google Fonts (Montserrat)
  • Custom CSS and JavaScript for branding and interactivity
  • Responsive images and SVG graphics

Summary & Next Steps

  • Website is ready for users: informative, attractive, and easy to use
  • Can be further expanded with more dynamic features (e.g., student login, news updates)
  • Feedback from users will help guide future improvements
